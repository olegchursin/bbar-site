Club Room

Host your event with us

The Club Room’s welcoming setting is ideal for anything from an engagement celebration, to a corporate event. With plenty of room for seated guests as well as standing, or as the night progresses dancing, the Club Room has something for everyone. Dark crimson banquettes line the walls of the intimate room with vintage photography from the owner’s legendary club Area scattered throughout.
