Private Events

CASUAL, FORMAL, AND EVERYTHING IN BETWEEN

BBar and Grill has multiple bars for events both private and semi-private. The Club Room and Garden Bar are perfect to host anything from weddings to corporate gatherings and everything in between. BBar and Grill’s event spaces are not limited to these bars, there are many more options for all types of events. Please email reservations@bbarandgrill.com if there is any other information we could provide you with.

Club Room

The Club Room’s welcoming setting is ideal for anything from an engagement celebration, to a corporate event.

Garden Bar

The Garden Bar is our most popular venue, while most guests enjoy the Garden Bar in the summer, it is heated and open year round.

Patio

Our enclosed patio space offers a multitude of options ideal for all sorts of events, from small intimate gatherings to large celebrations.

Front Bar & Parlor

The Front bar greets you as soon as you walk in the door. The casual atmosphere creates an engaging and exciting vibe.

Outside Garden

Stepping off the bustling NYC streets one enters the tranquil garden as if being transported to the European style courtyard.

Dining Room

The hip and funky atmosphere with heavy wood beams soaring overhead is accompanied with new american cuisine.
