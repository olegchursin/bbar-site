Front Bar and Parlor

Host your event with us

The Front Bar greets you as soon as you walk in the door. The full bar boasts 7 tap lines, 10 wines by the glass, and over 65 liqours, as well as a full signature cocktail list. The casual atmosphere creates an engaging and exciting vibe.

Front Parlor seating is great for a casual get together. Coffee tables and large leather seats surround the tables. The parlor is conveniently located right next to our front bar. The parlor is perfect for those trying to socialize with their guests, while not cutting themselves off from the rest of the good times to be had.
